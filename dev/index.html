Telepathic Black PantherAutomatic Behavior & Event Tracking

It Stalks

If you click here opening a new window or here navigating away in this window the panther will know and record both as outbound navigation events. Follow your visitors behavior and actions on your site and then know where they are exiting to.

It Reads Your Visitor's Minds

Want to know who's reading your pages and blog posts? Want to know where visitors get confused? How many are filling out forms or are abandoning them? Their behavior is monitored so you can adjust your site's UX to help them.

It Reports to Google Analytics

So you don't need to manually make calls to Google Analytics or really learn any JavaScript, everything is taken care of for you. Of course for those more adventurous, or for those who need more control, you are welcome to use the functions from Telepathic Black Panther for your own needs and do your own reporting.

It's Easy to Get Started

It's super easy to install him. All you need to do is include the JavaScript file on your pages and after your Google Analytics embed, add var panther = new Tbp();. There are default events which will be automatically detected and reported to Google Analytics based on the contents of your pages and your user's actions.

Demo Form

A form abandonment event will be emitted if the visitor begins filling in a form, but then leaves the page before finishing.
